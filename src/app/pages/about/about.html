Nedumpullil Ayurveda – Where Tradition Meets Wellness

Ayurveda is the oldest survival Medical Wisdom round the globe since 5000 years. This ever green medical wisdom is one among the best gifts of Indian civilization to alien humanity. Ayurveda as it word means the Veda of ‘Ayus’ or ‘Science of life.’ In short Ayurveda is defined as a system which uses inherent principles of nature which help to maintain health in an individual by keeping the body, mind and spirit in perfect equilibrium with nature. We Nedumpullil Ayurveda and Marma Chikitsa Kendram have a tradition of Ayurveda Practice through generations. We offer traditional Kerala Ayurvedic, Marma and Wellness therapies by preserving the tradition of Ayurveda with a modern outlook. We also offer a holistic wellness treatment with yoga and meditation.
